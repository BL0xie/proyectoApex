Description

Legends are the playable characters of Apex Legends and Apex Legends Mobile, each different from the other due to their abilities, playstyle, personality, and backstory. A new Legend may be introduced when a new Season starts.

Each Legend is defined by their unique Tactical ability, Passive ability, and Ultimate ability, the following abilities however are shared across the board, although some Legends may enhance one or more of these:

  • Each Legend is part of a class, which are categorized between Offensive.svg Offensive, Defensive.svg Defensive, Recon.svg Recon, and Support.svg Support.
  • Legends can walk, sprint, jump, crouch, crouch walk, slide, mantle, and climb walls up to 5-6 meters tall;
  • Through the use of their jump kits, Legends can skydive from high altitudes, boost themselves along zip lines, and negate fall damage;
  • Legends can equip up to two firearms and one of each kind of gear;
  • Legends have 10 inventory slots that can be used to carry ammo, ordnance, regen items, and spare attachments;
  • Legends can perform melee attacks at any time, which slightly knock back the enemy while also dealing damage;
  • Legends don't die immediately when depleting all of their health, instead they enter a Bleeding Out state where they may crawl around to safety. Depleting all their health while Bleeding Out however leads to actual death;
  • After dying, Legends drop their Death Box which contains all the items the Legends had, which can be accessed with a small delay. As well as their loot, Legends also drop their Legend Card, which allows their alive teammates to bring back the dead Legend into the arena by using a Respawn Beacon.svg Respawn Beacon, but it must be collected within a certain time limit, otherwise the Legend is out for good;
  • Legends can perform Finishers on Bleeding Out enemies, which completely refill their Body Shield.svg Body Shield while also killing the enemy.
